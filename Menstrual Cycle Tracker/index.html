Precautions & Self-Care

  • Maintain a healthy diet and drink plenty of water.
  • Get enough rest and sleep during your period.
  • Practice light exercise or yoga to reduce cramps.
  • Change sanitary products regularly to avoid infections.
  • Track symptoms to discuss with a doctor if needed.
  • If cycles are irregular for a long time, seek medical advice.

About This App

This menstrual cycle tracker helps you predict your next period date and fertile window based on your last period and cycle length.

Note: These predictions are only estimates. Always consult a healthcare professional for accurate advice.
